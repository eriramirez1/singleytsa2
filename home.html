Our Techology Track

Technology and Support Services: This class is basically Tech support; it involves four main courses which span across four years. (All of Highschool) These include the first track: Principles of Information Technology and Web Technologies. The second: Computer Maintenance/Lab. The third: Networking/Lab. And the final course which is Computer Technician Practicum/Extended.
Robotic Manufacturing: This class is the production and teaching of robots! It also goes through a span of four years, the first being: Principles of Robotic Manufacturing. The second: Manufacturing Engineering Technology I AND Robotics I. The third: Manufacturing Engineering Technology II AND Robotics II. And the final year: Practicum in Robotics and Manufacturing.
Video Game Design: In video game design you also spend four years in the class. The first is: Video Game Design and Web Technologies. The second: Computer Programming I AND Video Game Design II AND Video Game Design III. The third: Computer Programming II AND Animation I. And the final year: Animation II / Lab.
Geographic Information Systems: This is the last and weirdest of all the technology classes. It only has a span of three years which include: (1) Geographic Information Systems and Rastor-Based Geographic Information Systems, (2) Practicum in Information Technology, and the final class (3) Computer Technician/Practicum/Extended.
